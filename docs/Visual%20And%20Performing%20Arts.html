Visual And Performing Arts

This has all the subfields within “Visual And Performing Arts”. This field is for “Instructional programs that focus on the creation and interpretation of works and performances that use auditory, kinesthetic, and visual phenomena to express ideas and emotions in various forms, subject to aesthetic criteria.”
