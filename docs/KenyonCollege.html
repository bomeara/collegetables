The more “blue” the better the school is relative to its peers, the more “red” the worse. I keep with the convention that a lower acceptance rate is better (the school is more “selective”), as this may be an honest signal of student demand, but one could argue that not being able to serve many students (and yet often charging them to apply) is actually not great.
