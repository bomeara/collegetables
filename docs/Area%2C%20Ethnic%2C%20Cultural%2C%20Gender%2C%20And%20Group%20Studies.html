Area, Ethnic, Cultural, Gender, And Group Studies

This has all the subfields within “Area, Ethnic, Cultural, Gender, And Group Studies”. This field is for “Instructional programs that focus on the defined areas, regions, and countries of the world; defined minority groups within and across societies; and issues relevant to collective gender and group experience.”
