Wellesley College

Wellesley College is located in Wellesley, Massachusetts. It is a private not-for-profit, 4-year or above institution. Carnegie classifies it overall as being among “Baccalaureate Colleges: Arts & Sciences Focus” institutions, with its undergraduate program “Arts & sciences focus, no graduate coexistence” and graduate program “Not classified (Exclusively Undergraduate)”.
